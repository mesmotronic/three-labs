Three Labs

Welcome to Mesmotronic's Three Labs, where we just have some fun with Three.js and put on the web in case anyone finds it useful or entertaining

  • IOTA Balls - What would iota.macrocosmos.ai look like if we implemented all the change requests we received from a 5 year old? (ray marching)
  • Monkey See - A few hundred monkeys watching your every move (instanced mesh)
  • Point Magnets - A point cloud that follows your pointer(s) around... or tries to get away from them (points, raycasting)
  • RetroPass - Enjoiy a retro gaming video by sending your projects back to the 90s with RetroPass (post-processing)
  • Space Lazer VR - Strap on your VR headset and blast those 'roids! (WebXR)
