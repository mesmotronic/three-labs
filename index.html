Fun with Three.js

Just @neilrackett (@mesmotronic) having fun with Three.js and sticking the results online in case it's useful to anyone

  • IOTA balls - What would iota.macrocosmos.ai look like if I implemented a 5 year old's change requests? (ray marching)
  • Monkey see - A few hundred monkeys watching your every move (instanced mesh)
  • Point magnet - A point cloud that follows your mouse around... or tries to get away from it (points, raycasting)
  • Retro gaming - Send your projects back to the 90s with RetroPass (post-processing)
  • Space Lazer VR - Strap on your VR headset and blast those 'roids! (WebXR)
